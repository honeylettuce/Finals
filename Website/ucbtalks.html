Manoktok

Avoid opening the door to unknown visitors, especially during the night. Manoktok, people dressed in black hoods, were believed to bring ominous news during the war, often announcing deaths in families. To ward them off, people began painting white crosses on doors. When the war damaged most buildings, including homes, the Manoktok disappeared as there were no more doors to knock.

Pasundayag

The program serves as a warm embrace to usher students into the new school year, simultaneously paying homage to the celebration of Buwan ng Wika. This annual commemoration, held under the theme 'Dayun Pag-abi-abi ug Pagkahiusa sa Pasundayag sa UC-B,' beautifully encapsulates the essence of unity and cultural appreciation within the University of Cebu - Banilad community.
