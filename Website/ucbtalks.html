INTRAMURALS

The Banilad Intramurals is a community event promoting sports, teamwork, and camaraderie. Residents or members participate in various sports and recreational activities, fostering a sense of unity and healthy competition. The event aims to encourage an active lifestyle, build connections, and create a festive atmosphere with both traditional and creative activities.

Educational

The UC Banilad campus offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including business, engineering, information technology, health sciences, and liberal arts. Committed to academic excellence, UC Banilad provides a holistic education with experienced faculty, modern facilities, and opportunities for extracurricular activities. Students benefit from a dynamic learning environment, preparing them for success in their chosen fields.
